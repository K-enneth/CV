About me


I am Kenneth or just Kenny, since I was very young I have had a knack for maths and programming, I've always really liked video games and I have dreamed of being able to make one since i can remember.

I am studying Interactive Software and Video Game Development at Amerike, where I have learned to program in C# and Javascript, to use Unity, to model in Blender and to create VFX using shaders and particle systems in Unity.

I hope to learn and master as much as I can about game development and be able to work at studios that are as passionate about video games as I am, whether as a game developer, in gdame design or in VFX.
